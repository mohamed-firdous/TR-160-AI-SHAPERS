The Roman Empire was the post-Republican period of ancient Rome. As a polity, it included large territorial holdings around the Mediterranean Sea in Europe, North Africa, and Western Asia, ruled by emperors. From the accession of Augustus as the first Roman emperor to the military anarchy of the third century, it was a principate with Italy as the metropole of its provinces and the city of Rome as its sole capital.The Roman Empire was the post-Republican period of ancient Rome.
